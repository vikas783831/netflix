Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

  1. Go to Netflix login page
  2. Click Need help?
  3. Enter account email
  4. Check email for reset link

Why is Netflix buffering?

Common fixes:

  • Check internet speed (needs 3+ Mbps)
  • Restart your router
  • Lower video quality in settings

How to cancel subscription?

  1. Go to Account > Membership
  2. Click Cancel Membership
  3. Confirm cancellation

You can restart anytime within 10 months.

How to change video quality?

  1. Go to Account > Playback Settings
  2. Select quality level:
    • Auto (recommended)
    • Low (SD)
    • Medium (HD)
    • High (UHD)
  3. Save changes

Why can't I download shows?

Possible reasons:

  • Title not available for download
  • Device storage full
  • App needs update
  • Regional restrictions

How to use Netflix Party?

  1. Install browser extension
  2. Start playing a title
  3. Click extension icon
  4. Share link with friends

Why is my account logged out?

Common causes:

  • Password changed
  • Too many devices
  • Account security alert

How to get 4K content?

  • Upgrade to Premium plan
  • Use 4K compatible device
  • Ensure 25+ Mbps internet
  • Select "Ultra HD" in playback settings

How to remove viewing history?

  1. Go to Account > Profile
  2. Select Viewing Activity
  3. Click üö´ icon next to titles

Why is audio out of sync?

Troubleshooting steps:

  1. Restart device
  2. Check for app updates
  3. Try different audio track
  4. Contact your TV manufacturer
